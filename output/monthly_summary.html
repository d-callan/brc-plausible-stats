Notes

  • Organism Pages = /data/organisms/{tax_id} (individual organism detail pages)
  • Assembly Pages = /data/assemblies/{assembly_id} (individual assembly detail pages)
  • Workflow Pages = /data/assemblies/{id}/workflow-{...} (workflow configuration pages)
  • Index pages (Organisms Index, etc.) are navigation/listing pages, not detail pages
  • Community classification is based on NCBI taxonomy lineage
  • Unique Pages = number of distinct URLs visited that month
  • Visitors = unique visitors to those pages
  • Pageviews = total page loads (includes repeat visits)
  • Galaxy Workflow Landings = workflow landing requests received by Galaxy from BRC Analytics (tracked via Grafana/InfluxDB)
  • BRC Configs vs Galaxy Landings = comparison of workflow configuration pageviews in BRC vs actual workflow landings in Galaxy. Dashed lines = BRC, solid lines = Galaxy.
